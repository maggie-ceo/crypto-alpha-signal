Every field has a purpose

📌 Entry Zone — A bracket, not a single price. Reduces slippage and allows scaled-in accumulation.

✅ TP1–TP5 — Scaled exits. Close 40% at TP1, trail the rest. Locks profit while riding the move.

🛑 Stop-Loss — Hard exit. Never moved against you. Protects capital above all else.

⚠️ Invalidation — The structural level that voids the thesis. If price closes below this, the setup is dead regardless of SL.

Edge

Our Methodology

SMC institutional order flow combined with quantitative indicator confirmation.

📦 Order Blocks (OB)

Institutional accumulation/distribution zones. We identify where smart money entered and re-enter at the same levels.

🕳️ Fair Value Gaps (FVG)

Liquidity imbalances left by rapid moves. Algorithms pull price back to fill these — we position ahead of the fill.

💧 Liquidity Sweeps

Institutions hunt stop-loss clusters before reversing. We identify these sweeps and enter on the reversal, not the sweep.

📉 RSI Divergence

Price makes new highs while RSI makes lower highs — momentum exhaustion. One of the most reliable reversal signals.

📊 Bollinger Bands

Volatility envelope for breakout and mean-reversion confirmation. Band squeezes precede explosive moves.

🔄 BOS / CHoCH

Break of Structure confirms trend continuation. Change of Character signals early reversal — used for precision entries.
